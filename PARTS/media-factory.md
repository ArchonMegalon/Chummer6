# media-factory

## Summary
Render-only media lifecycle.

## What it owns
Render queues, storage adapters, signed URLs, dedupe/retry, rendered-asset lifecycle.

## What it does not own
Lore retrieval, session relay, provider routing, rules math.

## Why it matters now
This part exists to make the Chummer split real by shrinking old ownership and moving toward package-real or API-real boundaries.
