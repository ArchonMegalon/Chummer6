# Glossary

- **receipt**: the readable explanation of how a ruling or modifier was calculated
- **source trail**: where each rule, modifier, or published fact came from
- **local-first**: the important stuff keeps working even when the network gets stupid
- **preview**: visible and usable, but still moving toward its final public shape
- **current rules setup**: the exact rules, options, and package mix the session is using
- **ruleset**: the era or package of Shadowrun rules currently in play
- **prototype**: a preview build or page that is real enough to try, but still rough enough to bite
- **horizon**: a future idea worth tracking, not a promise
