## Meanwhile, Chummer is doing this

- grouping receipts into a readable review room
- separating grounded evidence from decorative trace noise
- keeping approval state attached to the proof
- making inspection feel like review instead of excavation

## Why that would be great

Proof becomes something humans can actually inspect without treating complexity as authority.

## Why it is still a Horizon

We're perfecting the engine's ability to sign its own work before we build the courtroom to display the evidence.

## What would need to exist first

- evidence receipts
- source classification
- approvals
- preview and apply separation
