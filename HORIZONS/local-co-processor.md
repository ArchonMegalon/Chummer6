## What problem does this solve?

## Why that would be great

## Why it is still a Horizon

Local acceleration is only a win if it remains optional. Until Chummer can prove that local compute improves the product without becoming a hidden requirement, this stays a horizon rather than a foundation promise.

## What would need to exist first

- portable deterministic engine host strategy
- hosted-first parity
- explicit non-mandatory local runtime policy
- disableable local acceleration paths
