## What problem does this solve?

Most runners spend three hours debating the ballistic rating of their trenchcoat and zero seconds checking if their spare clips actually fit their sidearms. Prep fails aren't usually a lack of courage; they’re a lack of basic self-respect. You shouldn't be realizing you forgot the medkit refills while you're already bleeding out in a Renraku shipping container.

## Why that would be great

Blackbox Loadout is the final sanity check before the street eats you. It scans your mission profile against your manifest to flag the 'oops' moments that burn Edge—missing smartlink cables, empty medkits, or that one specialty sensor you swore you bought. It’s an AR-powered gear-sync that understands context, ensuring your kit is actually lethal instead of just heavy.

## Why it is still a Horizon

We’re still decrypting the era-specific logic required to make this feel like a native tool instead of a glorified shopping list. The long-range plan needs a rock-solid rule engine first—because a gear-checker is useless if it doesn't know the difference between a legacy suppressor and a modern gas-vent system. It’s sitting in the data-haven, waiting for the core math to stabilize.
