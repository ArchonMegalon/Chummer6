## The short version

- the split is real
- the public surfaces are still preview, not the final public shape
- play is still the next major product seam to finish
- UI kit, registry, and media exist, but are still becoming fully real boundaries
- the blueprint is still catching up in a few places

## What that means for normal humans

We've stabilized the core SR4 support and hardened the offline-first session shell. Whether you're at a table in the Barrens or a high-rise in Downtown, you don't need a server connection to check your dice pools. The current build provides 'Math-Provenance'—popups that show the paper trail behind every modifier—so you can prove your bonuses to even the most suspicious GM without flipping through three different rulebooks.
