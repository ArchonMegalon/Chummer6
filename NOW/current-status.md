## What that means for normal humans

The toolkit is stable and ready for the table. You can install it as a local app on your device, kill the data connection, and still pull up a rule-legal runner dossier without a flicker. With support for multiple game eras starting with SR4, we've optimized the system for offline reliability, keeping your data on your hardware and out of the cloud. While the HTR team is pinging your position, your stats stay locked and loaded.
